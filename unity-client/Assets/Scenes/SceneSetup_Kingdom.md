# Kingdom.unity вЂ” Scene Setup Guide

## Purpose
Kingdom interior view. Shows the player's kingdom with all buildings
in fixed node positions. Camera is fixed isometric. Player can click
buildings to open upgrade/construct dialogs.

## Visual Target
Realistic medieval fantasy. Reference: League of Kingdoms, Rise of Kingdoms.
NOT cubes, NOT voxels, NOT Clash of Clans style.
